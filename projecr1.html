"The idea of 'Tail Globe' comes from the thought that the Earth we live on now was once home to many different creatures, some of which we see today and some we don't. Many species have faced extinction without our awareness. Therefore, if we can talk about and love the living beings around us, it can bring a significant change to nature and our lifestyle. I believe that the change we experience by raising a pet is substantial, and this belief is the foundation of this application."

"Through the Tail Globe app, you can find and sell your favorite pets. You can see the pets you like in person and buy them if you wish. You can also purchase food, medications, and grooming supplies needed for your pets. Additionally, the app will feature a pets gallery where you can upload photos and videos of your pet. If your pets fall ill, you can search for a good hospital and maintain a good contact through our app. Tail Globe will always be there to help keep your pets safe."

The idea behind "Aid for All" is indeed to simplify daily life and ensure that everyone can access resources and support equally. This initiative aims to address various needs—whether they are related to food, healthcare, education, or other essential services—so that individuals from all backgrounds can benefit. By focusing on accessibility and inclusivity, the goal is to create a more equitable society where everyone has the opportunity to thrive.

With the Eyed For All app, you can meet your daily needs from the comfort of your home. Here’s how it works:- Convenience at Your Fingertips: Whether you need groceries, home repairs, or medical assistance, the app connects you with service providers closest to your location. - Medical Assistance: If you’re not feeling well, you can easily contact a doctor through the app for guidance and support. - Home Services: Need a plumber or electrician? Instead of searching endlessly, simply find and book professionals directly through the app. - Job Opportunities: By listing your job type on the app, you can become a member and help reduce unemployment by offering your services to those in need. Download the Eyed For All app today and make life easier while providing or receiving essential services!
